Delicious Pancake

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoon melted butter

Instructions

  1. in a bowl , mix flour, sugar, baking powder and salt
  2. in another bowl, beat egg with milk and melted butter
  3. combine wet and dry ingredients and mix until smooth
  4. Heat a skillet and pour in batter to form pancakes
  5. Cook untill bubbles form, then flip and cook untill golden

Enjoy ypur homemade pancake!
